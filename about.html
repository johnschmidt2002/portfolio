About Me

Hello! I'm John Schmidt

I'm a Data Analyst with expertise in Python, SQL, and sports analytics.

My journey in data analytics and sports has been shaped by my passion for understanding the world through numbers and narratives. From a young age, I was drawn to statistics, not just as numbers but as stories waiting to be told. My love for sports has played a significant role in my career path, driving me to explore data-driven insights in athletic performance, game theory, and strategic decision-making.

Beyond my professional interests, I value strong relationships and the impact of faith in my life. These principles guide my decisions, keeping me grounded in moments of success and adversity. Whether analyzing a dataset, discussing a sports game, or engaging in meaningful conversations, I strive to bring curiosity, wisdom, and kindness into everything I do.
